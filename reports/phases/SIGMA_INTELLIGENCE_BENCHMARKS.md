# SIGMA_INTELLIGENCE_BENCHMARKS

10 measurable tests tracking cognitive development across system usage.

## Benchmark Suite

**1. Learning_speed**
- Measures how quickly new concepts form from observations
- Tracks first-success time for new patterns
- Normalized by input complexity

**2. Retention**
- Ability to recall and apply learned concepts over time
- Measures concept stability through decay periods
- Retention rate computed over time intervals

**3. Belief_accuracy**
- Agreement between high-confidence beliefs and ground truth
- Tracking calibrated confidence versus actual accuracy
- Updates as new validation data arrives

**4. Prediction_accuracy**
- Compares simulated outcomes with actual results
- Measures calibration of prediction confidence
- Aggregated across mental simulations

**5. Concept_quality**
- Evaluates clarity, stability, and utility of concepts
- Based on predictive power and generalization
- Cross-validated across domains

**6. Transfer_success**
- Measures when principles transfer to new domains
- Success defined by predictive accuracy post-transfer
- Tracks cross-domain learning efficacy

**7. Theory_accuracy**
- Compares leading theories against ground truth mechanisms
- Tracks theory emergence and validation rates
- Measures competing theory quality

**8. Reasoning_efficiency**
- Time and resources per reasoning step
- Compares solution quality to computational cost
- Identifies optimization opportunities

**9. Self_improvement_rate**
- Improvement magnitude after each governance-approved change
- Tracks composite metric improvement over time
- Measures return on improvement investment

**10. Simulation_quality**
- Fidelity of mental simulations to real outcomes
- Measures scenario coverage and accuracy
- Evaluates pessimistic/optimistic bias correction

## Composite Score

Combined score provides overall intelligence measure, weighted by importance and current phase.
